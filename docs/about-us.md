# About Us
We are a Discord community originally formed on Materia, passionate about any and all Savage and Extreme fights, no matter how long ago it released.

# Why does XIVMine exist?
This website exists to provide a centralized, easily accessible location for updated raidplans, guides, and resources for older FFXIV content. It addresses the lack of up-to-date and easily accessible information on older raids and extreme trials, ensuring that players have access to accurate, reliable, and clear resources. The site not only offers updated strategies and guides all in one place, but also preserves older resources that still hold value, giving credit to the original creators.
